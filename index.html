Campaign

This Is Circus

A corporate campaign reframing circus as a powerful lens for business — produced for NICA Represents, NICA's corporate entertainment and team-building arm.

The campaign challenged perceptions of circus as purely theatrical, repositioning it as a vehicle for leadership, resilience, and teamwork. Ran across Meta platforms with targeted reach to Melbourne's corporate sector.

The campaign strategy was presented to the Committee for Melbourne as part of a broader pitch to deepen corporate engagement with the arts ecosystem.

Show Campaign

ADRIFT

The 2025 NICA graduate showcase — a campaign built on softness and delicacy. Adrift captured the tender, ephemeral quality of a graduating class stepping into the world.

The visual identity drew from flowing, atmospheric imagery. A photoshoot produced assets used across show promotions, EDMs, and a suite of individual student Instagram posts — each with curated music chosen to mirror the feeling of each piece.

Every touchpoint was treated as part of one cohesive story: from the first poster spotted on a noticeboard to the final email sitting in someone's inbox.
